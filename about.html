Interests



I am interested in programming, 3D modelling, digital art, 'analogue' art, worldbuilding, consuming multimedia, the topic of time travel, listening to music, the creation of music, science in general, the (potential) science/fictional pseudoscience behind superpowers and supernatural abilities.


Moreso on the subject of programming, I am particularly interested in a few particular paradigms and types.


These paradigms are:


  • Imperative — The programmer instructs the machine how to change its state
    • Object-Oriented — Groups instructions with the part of the state they operate on
      • and
    • Procedural — Groups instructions into procedures
  • Declarative — The programmer merely declares properties of the desired result, but not how to compute it
    • Functional — The desired result is declared as the value of a series of function applications
      • and
    • Logic — The desired result is declared as the answer to a question about a system of facts and rules
    • and
  • Event-Driven — The flow of the program is determined by events such as user actions, sensor outputs or message passing from other programs or threads

The other types of programming I am interested in are the way they are Typed.
That is, whether they are Strongly or Weakly typed, and whether they are Statically or Dynamically typed.
