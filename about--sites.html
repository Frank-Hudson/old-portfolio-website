Entertainment



Entertainment websites are those which provides users with any form of fun distraction. This could include a video/music streaming or a 'Digital Videogame Distribution Service' website.


  • YouTube : https://www.youtube.com/

  • Spotify : https://www.spotify.com/

  • Steam : https://store.steampowered.com/

  • Epic Games : https://www.epicgames.com/



Activity



Activity websites are those that are more interactive than your typical site. They usually have some sort of activity for you to perform, like quizzes or mini-games.


  • Kahoot! : https://kahoot.it/

  • Quizizz : https://quizizz.com/join/

  • 16Personalities : https://www.16personalities.com/



Knowledge & Education



A 'Knowledge & Education' website is any website that is specificaly for the purpose of educating people.


  • NHS : https://www.nhs.uk/

  • Wikipedia : https://wikipedia.org/   *



Programming



A 'programming' website is a site that has a specific focus on programming and software development.
